*Yummy Strawberry Smoothie Recipe

Perfect for busy people!

Delicious smoothie recipe coming right up!

Ingredients:

  • 1 container of strawberry yogurt (any kind)
  • 3/4 cups low-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/4 cup ground oats
  • 1 teaspoon flax seed meal
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps to follow:

  1. Blend yogurt, milk, strawberries, oats, flax, and vanilla extract in a blender until smooth, about 30 seconds.
  2. Enjoy your quick smoothie!
*Please do not consume this smoothie if you have severe strawberry allergy.
